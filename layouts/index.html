share: social media share buttons for hugo.

This module facilates the sharing of content from Hugo websites on social platforms. After adding it to your site, you can add share buttons for popular social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and more.

STRAIGHTFORWARD

Minimal Configuration

Very little to no configuration is needed. The module sets sharing information for your content based on Hugo site and page variables. Only some platforms may require minimal configuration in cases where necessary information is not available from the aforementioned variables.
